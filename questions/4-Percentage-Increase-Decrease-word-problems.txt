Topics: Percentage Increase / Decrease, Original Price
-------------------------------------------------------


1) Damelza's salary of £24,500 is increased by 15%. What is her new salary?

2) 20% VAT is added to the basic price of a TV to give the selling price. The basic
price of the TV is £485. What is the selling price?

3) David's height increased by 20% between the ages of 6 and 10. He was 50 inches
tall when he was 6. How tall was he when he was 10?

4) At the start of a journey, Natalie's car had 8 gallons of fuel, and Jason's car
had 12 gallons of fuel. 
During the journey, Natalie used 25% of her fuel, and Jason used 40% of his fuel. Who
had more fuel left at the end of the journey? By how much?

5) Last year, Elsie's gas bill was £480 and her electricity bill was £612. This year,
her gas bill has increased by 2% and her electricity bill has decreased by 4%. Which is
now the more expensive bill? By how much?

6) Find the percentage increase when:

a) a price of £10 is increased to £12 
b) a price of £120 is increased to £174 

7) Find the percentage decrease when:

a) a price of £10 is decreased to £8 
b) a price of £150 is decreased to £138

8) The price of a local newspaper increases from 80p to £1.
Find the percentage increase.

9) A shop owner buys a pair of trainers for £52 and sells them for £70.20.
What is her percentage profit?

10) During a season, the average attendance for a local sports team's matches was 11350.
The following season, the average attendance was 11123.
Find the percentage decrease. 

11) A fridge costs £200 after a 50% reduction. 
Calculate the original price of the fridge.

12) An estate agent reduced the price of a house by 4%.
If the house is now for sale at £192,000, what was its original price?
